Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 3, 2026

JKAPPs ("we," "our," or "us") operates the WORDaGRAM: Neon Edition mobile application. This policy details how data is handled by our local game systems and the third-party Google services we utilize.

1. Third-Party Services & Technical Data

We utilize industry-standard services from Google to ensure game stability and provide advertisements. These services collect anonymized technical identifiers but do not have access to your personal game achievements or name:

  • Google AdMob & UMP: Collects device identifiers to manage user consent and serve advertisements based on your preferences.
  • Firebase Analytics: Collects anonymized telemetry (e.g., app open frequency, round starts). This data is aggregated and does not identify you personally.
  • Firebase Crashlytics: Collects technical logs and stack traces when the app encounters an error to help us provide bug fixes.

2. User Choices and Opt-Outs

You have full control over your privacy through the following methods:

  • Consent Management: A Google UMP consent dialog is presented at launch for you to choose your ad preferences.
  • System Settings: You can reset your Advertising ID or opt out of personalized ads at any time via your device:
    Settings > Google > Ads.
  • Total Opt-Out: To stop all technical data collection by third-party services, you may uninstall the application.

3. Local and Exclusive Storage

The following Personal Achievement Data is stored locally and exclusively on your device’s internal storage:

  • Individual high scores and player initials.
  • Daily check-in streak progress.
  • Game session history.

This specific achievement data is never transmitted to JKAPPs or any external servers (including Firebase). It remains strictly on your device to facilitate the offline leaderboard and streak features. Clearing your app cache or uninstalling the app will permanently delete this data.

4. Assets and Licenses

Audio: All sound effects in the application are programmatically synthesized and are original content created for this game.

Dictionaries: English word lists are provided via open-source projects including SCOWL, Hunspell, dwyl, and the English Open Word List (EOWL) under their respective licenses.

5. Security

We rely on the security protocols of Google and Firebase to protect any technical data processed by their services. However, no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.
